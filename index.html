How do I choose between centrifugal and self-priming pumps?

Centrifugal pumps are ideal for consistent water supply and high-flow applications. Self-priming pumps are best for tube wells and variable water levels. For agricultural irrigation in Gujranwala and Punjab, self-priming pumps are often preferred. Our team can recommend the right choice for your specific application.

Are S.Gold motors suitable for agricultural use in Pakistan?

Yes, S.Gold motors are specifically engineered for agricultural applications across Pakistan. They're designed for tube well pumping, irrigation systems, and harsh field conditions common in Punjab, Sind, and Balochistan.

What power capacity should I choose for my water pump?

Power requirements depend on your water depth, required flow rate, and distance. For tube wells in Gujranwala region: 1-5 HP for shallow wells, 5-15 HP for medium depth, 15-30 HP for deep wells. Consult our experts for a proper assessment.
