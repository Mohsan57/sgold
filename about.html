Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

S.Gold Electric Motors was founded in Gujranwala — Pakistan's industrial heartland. From our roots in Rajkot, we set out with a simple mission: to manufacture electric motors and water pumps that Pakistani farmers and industrialists could truly rely on.

Over the years, we've grown to serve more than 13,000 clients across Punjab and beyond. Our products power tube wells in rural villages, drive machinery in factories, and keep irrigation systems running through the harshest seasons.

Every motor and pump that leaves our facility is tested to meet strict quality standards. We believe that when your livelihood depends on a machine, that machine must never let you down.
