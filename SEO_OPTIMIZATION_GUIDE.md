### 6. **Technical SEO Files**

#### **robots.txt** (Created)
```
User-agent: *
Allow: /
Sitemap: https://sgold.pk/sitemap.xml
```
- Allows all search engines to crawl
- Blocks harmful bots (AhrefsBot, SemrushBot)
- References sitemap for better indexing

#### **sitemap.xml** (Created)
```xml
- Home: Priority 1.0 (updated weekly)
- Products: Priority 0.9 (updated weekly)
- About: Priority 0.8 (updated monthly)
- Contact: Priority 0.8 (updated yearly)
```
- Helps Google find all pages
- Indicates update frequency for crawl priority
